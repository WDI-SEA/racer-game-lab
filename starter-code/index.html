How To Play

Requires 2 players. Player 1 is assigned the pink runner. To move the pink runner towards the finish line, hit the 'a' key repeatedly. Player 2 is assigned the green runner. To move the green runner towards the finish line, press the 'l' key repeatedly. The first runner to the finish line.

Please note: This game is best viewed with your browser at 1250px wide.
